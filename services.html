Our Services

At Concussion Firearms, we offer a variety of services to meet your firearm needs. Whether you’re looking to purchase, transfer, or order a firearm, we are here to ensure a smooth and seamless experience. As a veteran-owned and locally rooted business, we are dedicated to providing excellent service to our community and fellow veterans.

Firearm Sales

At Concussion Firearms, we offer a wide selection of high-quality firearms to suit all needs, whether you're a seasoned enthusiast or a first-time buyer. We pride ourselves on providing excellent customer service, expert advice, and top-notch products from trusted manufacturers. As a veteran-owned business, we understand the importance of having a reliable firearm, and we're here to help you make the right choice.

Firearm Transfers

We offer seamless firearm transfer services to ensure you can legally acquire and transfer firearms with ease. Whether you're buying from a private seller or another FFL (Federal Firearms License) holder, we’ll guide you through the transfer process to ensure everything is done by the book. We make firearm transfers fast, easy, and stress-free for our customers.

Special Orders

Looking for something specific? Concussion Firearms can help you place special orders for firearms that aren’t currently in our inventory. If you're interested in a particular make, model, or brand, we'll do our best to fulfill your order at a great price. With our extensive network of suppliers, we can help you find exactly what you need.

Customer Support

At Concussion Firearms, we believe in offering more than just products – we provide personalized customer support to ensure you're making the best decisions for your needs. Whether you're new to firearms or an experienced collector, we’ll walk you through your options, answer your questions, and help you find the firearm that fits your needs. Our veteran-owned and operated business is here to serve you with respect, dedication, and expertise.
