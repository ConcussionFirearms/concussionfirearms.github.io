Who We Are

At Concussion Firearms, we are more than just a firearms business – we are a proud veteran-owned and locally rooted company serving the Cheyenne community. Founded by a U.S. Military veteran, Concussion Firearms was built on the principles of duty, honor, and service. We understand the value of trust and dedication, which is why we treat each customer with the same respect and care that we would expect for ourselves.
